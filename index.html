About Me

Hi! My name is Jefferson but you can call me Jeff. I'm 26 years old and I live with my princess Stella (dog), in Orange, New Jersey. I grew up in Heredia not far from the capital of Costa Rica. When I turned eight, my family and I moved to the United States. My hobbies include art, hydroponics, wood work, camping, canoeing, and meeting new people. My friend Jasper, a computer science major, opened my interest in coding when we tried to start a company together called Bluekiwii with no success. A few years later I realized I still had the desire to pursue what I started. So, I enrolled in a class Rutgers Coding Bootcamp and here I am. I work as a foreman in a painting company where I oversee 6 employees, make proposals, and deal with clients. I am open for new advantures and challege
